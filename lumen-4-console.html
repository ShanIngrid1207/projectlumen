[ 04 ] process.log

How a build runs. Five stages, one tail -f.

Most projects ship in four to six weeks. You see progress on a live URL the whole way, not in a slide deck at the end.

week 1 · 09:14 · discover

Discover

We read your analytics, talk to your team, and write down what the site actually has to do. Scope is fixed before any pixels move.

week 1–2 · design

Design

Type, color, and layout, prototyped in the browser. You react to real screens, not mockups, and we iterate in days, not weeks.

week 2–4 · build

Build

Front-end code, CMS wiring, and content load-in on a staging URL you can open any time. Daily commits, visible progress.

week 4–5 · launch

Launch

Performance pass, accessibility audit, analytics, and DNS cutover. We watch the first 48 hours so you do not have to.

ongoing · care

Care

Hosting, updates, and monitoring after launch. When the site needs a change, you email a person, and a person answers.
